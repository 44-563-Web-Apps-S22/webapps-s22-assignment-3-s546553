Dharmapuri Daredevil

Dharmapuri Daredevil is a sport mascot for my favourite game Cricket in my hometowm.This bird is small and tiny with golden yellow feathers flying high in the sky spreading its wings up in the air is always considered auspicious in my culture. The birds is very beautiful and always created enthusiasim and motivates the team spirit.Whenever we see the bird we are delighted and our hearts filled with joy.The bird name is Asian Golden Weaver.


Funfacts about Dharmapuri Daredevil

  1. Always travels in group
  2. flies high and dives towards the earth
  3. Eats small worms and fishes
