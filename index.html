I favourite place to buy food is Hotel Shadab in Hyderabad,India.

Located in the heart of this incredible city of Hyderabad, Shadab Hotel is the epitome of everything Hyderabad. Shadab was establsihed in 1990, by a Hyderabadi family with one motive in mind - Elegance through simplicity. It is located very close to the iconic Bhagya Laxmi Temple in Charminar , India and also the famous monument charminar is located adjacent to BhagyaLaxmi temple.

Directions to get to Hotel Shadab from Airport

Closest airport to Hotel Shabad is Begumpet airport.

  1. To get to shadab from airport , take a shuttle no.71 outside the airport.
  2. Getdown at amberpet mahankali temple.
  3. Get into a sharing auto which will cost around rs.20/-
  4. Follow below steps.
    1. Take a ticket to Madina building.
    2. From madina building take a ride to chatrinaka
  5. At chatrinaka take left and walk 200m to reach to Shadab Hotel.
